About Us

Who we are

The Side Band is Boise’s feel-good cover group, blending classic rock, worship, and 90s favorites into an energetic live experience.

The band

Founded by friends with a shared passion for worship music and live performances, The Side Band brings a polished yet hometown feel to every venue. We believe in music that invites people to sing along, celebrate, and feel at home.

From private parties to public concerts, our goal is always the same: deliver an engaging, memorable set that keeps people dancing and makes every event sound bigger than it is.

Why we do it

  • • Honest live music with a strong community feel.
  • • A curated setlist of worship, classics, and crowd-pleasing hits.
  • • Reliable, professional performance and friendly stage energy.
